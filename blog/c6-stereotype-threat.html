Today is the first time that I've ever heard of stereotype threat. After learning more about this phenomenon, I don't think that I've ever felt it to be honest. I also haven't given the topic much thought either. I can't imagine how it must feel though, to feel like you are confirming society's negative expectations of your status class or ethnic group. I'm sure that quite a lots of peple experience stereotype threat everyday, but don't really talk about it. I think that if they were to discuss it to someone outside of their group, they would feel like they are reinforcing the negative stereotype even further. So the condition continues to be exacerbated while nothing is done to address the situation.

My advice would be to seek someone out who you think might be facing this problem and bring it up slowly. If you can find a few people, you guys can create a group to discuss these issues. This can be a safe place where others can voice their insecurities in a safe place without any fear of judgement.
